Quiz Game Help

Level Selection:

To select the left option, press "A" key or click on the left arrow. To select the right option, press "D" key or click on the right arrow. Alternatively, you can tilt your mobile device to the left to choose the left option and tilt it to the right to choose the right option.

Game Rules:

The game consists of 5 levels, and each level has 10 questions. You have 5 seconds to answer each question. If you answer a question incorrectly or the time runs out, the game will restart from the beginning.
